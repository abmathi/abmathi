Since I first picked up a camera, I’ve been drawn to two extremes: the buzz of the street and the stillness of the outdoors. My photography is about presence — capturing the unposed, unscripted beauty of daily life, whether it’s a neon-lit alley or a silent ridge at dawn.

This is why I shoot. Not to control a scene, but to witness it. To be a part of something fleeting.

Thanks for following along.

— Austin
