Hi, I'm Austin — a photographer with a go-with-the-flow, worry-free vibe, capturing moments from gritty streets to serene hiking trails. I shoot with a Nikon Z6 II and a trusty 40mm f/2 lens.

I've always dreamed of being featured in National Geographic, and *Austin’s Angle* is where I share that journey — raw, real, and unposed.

Whether it’s late-night city corners or golden light in pine forests, this is my angle on the world.

Follow me on [Instagram](https://instagram.com/austinsangle) and [YouTube](https://youtube.com/@austinsangle) for more behind-the-scenes.
